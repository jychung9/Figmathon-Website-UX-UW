About Figmathon

This year’s UX@UW hackathon is a virtual 2 day designathon held on Zoom. We aim to bring together people with diverse skill sets to develop creative solutions that empower and uplift us into brighter and more equitable future.

Our theme for this designathon is - Design the future. Within the past two years, we’ve been through a pandemic, massive protests for justice and equality, the ever increasing number of natural disasters around the world, among others. With some of the life-changing events that have taken place, we encourage you to rethink how you want a more equitable future to look like.

UX@UW invites students and non-students form all backgrounds and identities to join in on this experience to celebrate a more equitable future through our hackathon.

Join the Discord for all hackathon updates and announcements!

Registration is first-come, first-served, so get your tickets today!

Awards

Best Design

This team considers both the UX and UI in their pitch. Their idea comes to life through aesthetic and intuitive visuals. The design is fully functional and showcases thoughtful consideration and intent behind each design decision.

Best Impact

This team hones inclusive and cognizant of a target user group in considering the positive impact of their idea on them. Their idea is innovative and unique but also considers the possible consequences of the design and puts in perspective their product in the context of broad social change.

Best Overall

This team does it all. Their project is aesthetic and functional and promotes positive future of change. Their idea promises feasibility, market potential, and positive impact.
